TREE PEOPLE ORGANIZATION

Tree People Organization is a nonprofit, non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Tamil Nadu, India, established in 2022. We are dedicated to creating a positive impact in the lives of the marginalized and underprivileged sections of society. Our work focuses on providing care and support to the elderly, ensuring education for underprivileged children, empowering women, and offering skill development programs to uplift communities.
