About Us

TREE PEOPLE CHARITABLE TRUST

Tree People Organization is a nonprofit, non-governmental organization (NGO) based in Tamil Nadu, India, established in 2022. We are dedicated to creating a positive impact in the lives of the marginalized and underprivileged sections of society. Our work focuses on providing care and support to the elderly, ensuring education for underprivileged children, empowering women, and offering skill development programs to uplift communities. We believe in the power of compassion and collective action to bring about sustainable change. Our team is driven by the vision of a society where every individual, regardless of age, gender, or socioeconomic status, has access to basic necessities, education, and opportunities to thrive.

Our Vision

Tribals
  • 1, Empowering tribal communities to thrive with dignity, self-sufficiency, and cultural pride.
  • 2, Bridging the gap between tribal communities and mainstream society through education, healthcare, and economic empowerment.
  • 3, Fostering inclusive and sustainable development in tribal regions, preserving cultural heritage and promoting social justice.

To build a compassionate and inclusive society where every individual, especially the elderly, children, and women, can live with dignity, hope, and opportunity.

Our Mission

  • 1.Elderly Care:Provide a safe and nurturing environment for the elderly through our old age home, ensuring they live with dignity and respect.
  • 2.Education for Children:Support underprivileged and orphaned children by enabling their access to quality education and a brighter future.
  • 3.Women Empowerment:Empower women through skill development programs, awareness campaigns, and support for economic independence.
  • 4.Community Development:Offer skill training programs to destitute individuals, helping them become self-reliant and contributing members of society.
  • 5.Support for the Destitute:Provide essential services and care for destitute individuals, ensuring they have access to food, shelter,and medical care.

Our Programs

Tribals

Education

- Tribal Literacy Program: Establishing community-based literacy centers for adults and children.

- Scholarship Scheme: Providing financial assistance to meritorious tribal students for higher education.

- Vocational Training: Offering skill development programs in areas like agriculture, handicrafts, and entrepreneurship.

Healthcare

- Mobile Health Clinics: Operating mobile health units to provide medical services in remote tribal areas.

- Health Awareness Campaigns: Conducting workshops and awareness programs on nutrition, sanitation, and disease prevention.

- Traditional Medicine Preservation: Documenting and promoting traditional tribal medicine practices.

Economic Empowerment

- Livelihood Support: Providing training and resources for tribal communities to develop sustainable livelihoods.

- Microfinance Initiatives: Establishing microfinance programs to support tribal entrepreneurs and small business owners.

- Market Access: Facilitating market access for tribal products, such as handicrafts and organic produce.

Cultural Preservation

- Cultural Documentation: Documenting tribal customs, traditions, and languages.

- Cultural Exchange Programs: Organizing cultural exchange programs to promote cross-cultural understanding and appreciation.

- Tribal Art and Craft Promotion: Promoting and preserving tribal art and craft forms.

Old Age Home Services

- A safe and caring home for the elderly, offering medical care, emotional support, and a community to thrive in.

- Regular health check-ups, recreational activities, and counselling services.

Education for Underprivileged Children

- Sponsorship programs for school fees, uniforms, and educational materials.

- After-school tutoring and mentorship programs to ensure academic success.

Orphan Care

- Providing shelter, food, and education to orphaned children.

- Emotional and psychological support to help them heal and grow.

Skill Development and Training*

- Vocational training programs in tailoring, handicrafts, computer skills, and more.

- Workshops to enhance employability and entrepreneurship opportunities.

Women Empowerment Initiatives

- Skill training programs tailored for women, including sewing, embroidery, and small business management.

- Awareness campaigns on health, hygiene, and legal rights.

Support for Destitute Individuals

- Providing food, shelter, and medical care to those in need.

- Rehabilitation programs to help them reintegrate into society.
